## 6. Merchant Deal Frameworks

### Framework 1: Lunch Volume Play

**Target:** Regular & Medium Spenders (16,414 users)

**Geography:** Central, Orchard, Marina Bay CBD

**Timing:** 11am-2pm SGT

**Deal Structure:**
- Min Spend: $10 (median lunch = $9)
- MPD Rate: 50-100 MPD (~$0.50-1.00)
- Best for: Fast casual, food courts, coffee + lunch

**Merchant Pitch:**
> "16,000+ users dine out for lunch every day. 40% of their dining happens 11am-2pm. We'll drive them to your restaurant during lunch rush for $0.50-1 per customer."

**Volume Projection:**
- 16,414 users √ó 13 avg lunch txns/90d = 213K potential transactions
- 1% conversion = 2,130 redemptions √ó $10 avg = $21,300 revenue
- Merchant cost: $1,065-$2,130 (5-10% cost-to-revenue)

### Framework 2: Dinner Premium Play

**Target:** All spend tiers (24,156 users) ‚Äî dinner is 37% of volume

- MPD Rate: 150-250 MPD (~$1.50-2.50)
- Best for: Sit-down restaurants, Japanese/Korean, Italian

**Merchant Pitch:**
> "Dinner transactions are 50% higher value than lunch ($45 vs $29 avg). 37% of dining happens 6-9pm. We'll drive high-value dinner customers to your restaurant."

**Volume Projection:**
- 24,156 users √ó 13 avg dinner txns/90d = 314K potential transactions
- 2% conversion = 6,280 redemptions √ó $35 avg = $220K revenue
- Merchant cost: $9,420-$15,700 (4-7% cost-to-revenue)

**Why This Works:**

- 50% higher transaction values ‚Üí better merchant ROI
