# HeyMax Company Vision

## Introduction

## Mission
HeyMax is a leading travel-tech company in the ad/loyalty/fin-tech industry. Our mission is to bring more joy and empathy to the world by making travel more accessible. We help people turn their everyday interactions into travel rewards - uniting brands and travellers through **Max Miles** - the consumer-centric universal travel reward.

## Vision
1. **Free travel for you** — funded by the businesses people already engage with.
2. **One wallet for every mile** — no more orphaned points or fragmented loyalty programs.
3. **Consumer-centricity brings business outcome **— businesses get higher ROI by building real relationships through the shared aspiration of travel.

## **HeyMax’s Value to Consumers and Businesses **

### Value for consumers: 
- Fastest way to free travel through everyday interactions with businesses.

### Value for businesses: 
- Leverage consumer-centric travel proposition to effectively acquire, engage, and retain travel-loving consumers.

## Company Values

### **Deliver Long-Term Customer Value**
- Every decision starts with our users. We listen, design, and iterate with their needs at the center.

### **Transparency**
- We believe trust is built on honesty. Whether it's our product, policies, or partnerships, we communicate openly and proactively so our team and users can rely on us to deliver exactly what we promise. We want users to trust in HeyMax every step of the way.

### **Be The Owner**
- Ownership is our mantra. More than just accountability, it's taking initiative, making informed decisions, and leading with integrity. We each own the impact we have on our customers, the well-being of our team, and the outcomes of our actions.

## Target Market

### **Consumer Segments:**
- **Mass-market travelers** seeking an emotionally rewarding, simple path to their next holiday
- **Rewards optimizers** who want confidence they're earning the smartest value without complexity
- **Cashback users** ready to graduate from small savings to meaningful travel acceleration

### **Business Segments:**
- **Merchant partners** (retailers, platforms, premium brands, subscription services) seeking performance-based growth without brand dilution
- **Redemption partners** (airlines, hotels, travel providers, experience operators) looking to improve capacity utilization through predictable demand

## Competitive Position

### **For Consumers:** 
HeyMax is positioned as the Travel Accelerator that bridges daily spending and real travel outcomes, with deep expertise in:
- **Travel acceleration** — making every dollar visibly move customers closer to their next holiday
- **Smart optimisation** — ensuring the highest rewards without mental load or MCC confusion
- **Value translation** — converting abstract miles into clear, motivating progress toward actual trips

### **For Merchant Partners:** 
HeyMax is positioned as a performance demand channel that drives efficient growth without devaluation, with deep expertise in:
- **Performance-based acquisition** — converting marketing spend into incremental sales at participating merchants
- **Brand equity preservation** — influencing purchase decisions without discounts or price anchoring
- **Attributable demand** — delivering measurable, incremental GMV with efficient CAC

### **For Redemption Partners:** 
HeyMax is positioned as a demand aggregation platform that creates predictable, high-intent redemptions, with deep expertise in:
- **Redemption acceleration** — shortening time between earning and redeeming through visible progress
- **Capacity optimisation** — channeling miles-based demand toward excess or low-yield inventory
- **Utilisation predictability** — building a consistent base of redemption-ready customers

## Core Message (The One Thing)
**Consumer:** "Get to your holiday sooner with every dollar spent."
**Merchant Partner:** "Performance-based demand without discounting your brand."
**Redemption Partner:** "Turn everyday spending into consistent, high-intent redemptions."

## Message Hierarchy
### Primary: Travel Accelerator (Emotional, Mass-Market)
The brand leads with acceleration — the visible momentum toward real travel that makes HeyMax feel rewarding and achievable for everyone.

### Secondary: Smart Optimizer (Rational, Credibility Layer)
The brand supports with optimization — the confidence-building tools that ensure users always earn the smartest rewards without effort.
